## Roadmap Governance

### Update Cadence
- **Weekly:** Status updates in team standup
- **Bi-weekly:** Detailed progress review
- **Monthly:** Stakeholder roadmap review
- **Quarterly:** Full roadmap refresh and reprioritization

### Prioritization Framework
We use **RICE scoring** (Reach × Impact × Confidence / Effort) with AI-assisted analysis for initial scoring, refined by team judgment.

### Change Management
- Minor changes: Product Owner approval
- Major changes: Steering committee review
- Emergency changes: Documented post-hoc
