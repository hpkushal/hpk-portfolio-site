## 3. Communication Channels & Cadence

### 游늵 Monthly
*   **Executive Steering Committee:**
    *   *Format:* 30-min Slide Deck (async-first option available)
    *   *Content:* High-level progress, risks, budget/resource asks, key decisions needed
    *   *Tools:* Google Slides, Loom recording for async

### 游댃 Bi-Weekly / Sprintly
*   **Sprint Review / Demo:**
    *   *Format:* Live Demo (recorded for async viewers)
    *   *Audience:* Engineering, Product, Design, optional Stakeholders
    *   *Content:* Demo of shipped work, metrics impact, next sprint preview
    *   *Tools:* Zoom, Loom, Notion

### 游닓 Weekly
*   **Project Status Update:**
    *   *Format:* Written Update (Email or Slack)
    *   *Audience:* All Stakeholders
    *   *Content:* 游릭/游리/游댮 status, blockers, upcoming milestones, help needed
    *   *Tools:* Slack #[project-name], Email digest
    *   *Template:*
    ```
    **Status:** 游릭 On Track / 游리 At Risk / 游댮 Blocked
    **Progress:** [Key accomplishments this week]
    **Blockers:** [Issues needing resolution]
    **Next Week:** [Upcoming focus areas]
    **Help Needed:** [Specific asks]
    ```

### 游눫 Ad-Hoc
*   **Slack Channel:** #[project-name] for daily updates, questions, and quick decisions
*   **1:1 Syncs:** With key influencers as needed (schedule proactively)
*   **AI-Assisted Summaries:** Use Claude/ChatGPT to summarize long threads for stakeholders

## 6. Meeting Hygiene
- All meetings have a clear agenda shared 24h in advance
- Meetings end with documented decisions and action items
- Default to 25/50 minute meetings (not 30/60)
- "No agenda, no meeting" policy
- Record important meetings for async participants

---

## 7. Feedback Mechanisms
- **Weekly pulse:** Quick emoji reaction in Slack on project health
- **Retrospectives:** End-of-sprint and end-of-project
- **Stakeholder survey:** Monthly for major initiatives
- **Open door:** Any stakeholder can raise concerns directly to PM
